About the Show

Every great achievement starts with someone willing to build.

The Builder's Handbook is a podcast exploring the ideas, decisions, and stories behind people building businesses, organizations, communities, technology, and lives of lasting impact.

Each episode features conversations with founders, executives, innovators, creators, pastors, investors, and thought leaders who share practical lessons for builders everywhere.

Whether you're leading a company, launching a startup, serving your community, or simply trying to build a meaningful life — this is your handbook.
